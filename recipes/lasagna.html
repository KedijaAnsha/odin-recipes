Lasagna

Description

This homemade lasagna is a comforting, cheesy dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil lasagna sheets until tender.
  3. Cook ground beef with garlic and olive oil, then add tomato sauce.
  4. Layer pasta, meat sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes until bubbly and golden.
